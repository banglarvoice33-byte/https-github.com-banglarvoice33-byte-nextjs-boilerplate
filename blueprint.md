# **App Name**: Banglar Pulse

## Core Features:

- AI Content Engine: An automated pipeline that acts as a tool to rewrite factual news summaries and perform SEO optimization for new articles.
- Automated E-Paper Generator: Nightly PDF generation of a 10-page digital newspaper featuring the top stories of the day across all major categories.
- News Grid Dashboard: A high-performance landing page featuring Breaking News, National, Politics, and Tech categories for quick news consumption.
- Telegram Control Panel: Real-time management system to monitor sync status and force-sync news sources directly via Telegram commands.
- Relational Data Management: Robust data architecture using PostgreSQL and Prisma to handle users, authors, tags, and articles.
- Admin Management Console: Centralized dashboard for content editors to perform fact-verification human reviews and manage site analytics.
- Offline PWA Support: Progressive Web App functionality enabling offline reading, background synchronization, and instant push notifications.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Deep Royal Blue (#2E4CB2) to evoke journalistic authority and professional trust.
- Background: Clean Pearl White (#F5F6F9) with light desaturated blue tones to reduce eye strain during long-form reading.
- Accent Color: Vibrant Cyan (#1BA1E2) for CTAs and status indicators, providing high-contrast against the primary blue.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for a traditional news masthead feel; Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for high legibility in dense article content.
- Classic multi-column editorial grid inspired by modern broadsheet aesthetics, optimizing information density while maintaining whitespace.
- Clean, thin-stroke SVG icons that reflect different news sectors like tech, sports, and international politics.
- Subtle top-to-bottom entrance transitions for new article cards and smooth pagination fades.
